Detailed Description

template<std::size_t KeyBits>
class nil::crypto3::block::camellia< KeyBits >

Camellia. A Japanese design standardized by ISO, NESSIE and CRYPTREC. Somewhat common. Comes in three variants, Camellia-128, Camellia-192, and Camellia-256. Prefer AES or Serpent in new designs.

Template Parameters
KeyBitsBlock cipher key bits. Supported values are: 128, 192, 256
